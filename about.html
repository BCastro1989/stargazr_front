About

Because you want to see ALL of the stars above you

We were just a few nerds met at a hackathon and made a star gazing map web app in a weekend. Stargazr uses a combination of open source APIs and datasets plus software tools and hackathon talent to find you the best night sky closest to you on a given night.

Resources:

  • Google Earth Engine
  • National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration VIIRS DNB Cloud Free Composites
  • Light Pollution and Science Technology Institute World Atlas
  • David Lorenz Light Pollution Atlas (2006 Satellite Adjusted)
  • Clear Dark Sky Charts
  • Spatial by Templated
